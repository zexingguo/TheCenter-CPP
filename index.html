Welcome to the Center for Information Assurance

The Center For Information Assurance is the focal point for Information Assurance and Cyber Defense Activities at Cal Poly Pomona. Since 2005 Cal Poly Pomona has been a Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Education designated by the National Security Agency and Department of Homeland Security.

Our Goal

We aim to bring together students and industry professionals to create an enhanced education experience.

CyberSecurity

We oversee cybersecurity education and outreach across campus and for our students.

Projects

We take Cal Poly Pomona's "learn-by-doing" approach and allow our students to be involved in hands-on projects.

Research

Our institute researchers cover topics ranging from networking security to computer ethics and the social impact of technology.

Events

Cyber Security

Cal Poly Pomona offers three majors related to cyber security, each with their own respective curriculum: Computer Information Systems, Computer Science, and Electrical and Computer Engineering.

Applied Research

Our campus institute researchers are involved in several interests, ranging from forensics to network security.

Student Organizations

Our campus takes pride in our students' involvement on campus through student organizations. There are several clubs available on campus based in all three curriculums.
